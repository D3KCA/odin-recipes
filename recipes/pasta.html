Pesto Pasta

Pesto pasta has always been a favorite of mine because of its perfect balance of fresh, vibrant flavors. The rich blend of basil, garlic, pine nuts, and Parmesan creates a deliciously fragrant sauce that's both creamy and light, making every bite feel fresh and satisfying. The olive oil adds a smooth texture that complements the al dente pasta, and the burst of herbal notes gives it a refreshing taste that never feels too heavy. It's a dish that's simple yet packed with flavor, and it always feels like a treat, whether as a quick weeknight meal or a special dinner.

Pesto Pasta Ingredients

These are the ingredients needed to create this recipe that is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold!

  • Pasta: Get your favorite pasta shape to start with!
  • Onion and oil:Cook the onions in the olive oil until translucent.
  • Pesto:Two options here Store-Bought or Homemade for your pesto sauce.
  • Seasonings: Keep it simple here stick with salt and pepper.
  • Cheese: Grate your own Parmesan cheese don't go pre-shredded stuff.

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
